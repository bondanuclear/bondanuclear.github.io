2048

Remake of classic 2048 game.

What's the point of the game?

Move the tiles until you get the 2048 value. If all the cells have value and you can't merge any of them - you lose. The more cells you merge the higher score you get.

Worth mentioning:

  • Learnt how to use Zenject.
  • Used object pool to spawn tiles.
  • Used game states to control the flow of the game.
